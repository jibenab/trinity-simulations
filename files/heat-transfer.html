Heat Transfer

§ 01 — Conduction
Heat travels through vibrating particles. No bulk movement of matter.
§ 02 — Convection
Intensity 50%
Heated fluid rises, cool fluid sinks — bulk movement carries energy in loops.
§ 03 — Radiation
Medium Vacuum
Energy travels as electromagnetic waves — no medium required.
Mode Medium Required Particle Motion Typical Example
