Pulley Puzzle

Build real pulley systems by placing pulleys, a load, and an effort point. Connect coloured nodes with rope, then check whether the machine meets the goal.

Goal: Pull down and lift up
Level 1 of 5
Tools
How To Play

Drag items to move them. Drag from a coloured node to another to connect rope. Release near the top beam to snap to a ceiling anchor. Double-tap a rope to delete it. Double-tap a pulley, load, or effort to remove it.
