How it works

  • Cohesion — intermolecular attraction between like molecules. High cohesion makes fluid form round droplets and resists spreading.
  • Adhesion — attraction between fluid and the glass tube walls. When adhesion > cohesion, fluid climbs the tube (concave meniscus — water). When cohesion > adhesion, fluid is repelled (convex meniscus — mercury).
  • Capillary Action — the balance of adhesion, cohesion and surface tension. See it live by switching between Water and Mercury presets.
